About Me

From a young age, I always found myself appreciating good design. Whether it was a beautiful and easy-to-navigate website, or a piece of furniture that was stylish while still being functionally comfortable, I felt drawn to well-designed products. Products should be designed with the user in mind, so that they immerse the user in an experience that brings them joy. Ease-of-use, comfort, convenience, and simplistic functionality are some of the main aspects on my mind when I am designing or evaluating a product.

I am currently a dedicated student in the Human Systems Engineering program with a concentration in User Experience at Arizona State University.

Passionate about crafting seamless and intuitive user experiences through the lens of human systems engineering, I thrive in designing innovative solutions that harmonize technology and user needs, elevating the digital landscape with a user-centric touch.
